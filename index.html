Voice to Mail Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Voice to Mail is a simple productivity application developed by Porflitt Apps. The app allows users to record their voice, convert speech into editable text, generate a short email subject, and open an editable email draft in the user's selected email application.

Information the app processes

Voice to Mail may process voice input in order to convert speech into text. This processing is performed through the speech recognition services available on the user's Android device. Depending on the user's device settings and speech recognition provider, voice processing may occur on-device or through services provided by the device or operating system provider.

Voice to Mail does not operate its own servers and does not store voice recordings, transcriptions, email content, or user messages on Porflitt Apps servers.

Information stored on the device

The app may store basic preferences locally on the user's device, such as:

  • Selected language
  • Selected email address for preparing the email draft
  • Audio preference, such as whether to mute other apps while recording

These preferences are used only to make the app work as expected.

Email content

Voice to Mail opens an editable email draft in the user's email application. The user can review, edit, or delete the content before sending. The app does not automatically send emails. Once the user sends an email, the message is handled by the user's email provider and email application.

Data sharing

Porflitt Apps does not sell personal data and does not share user data with advertisers. Voice to Mail does not include advertising SDKs.

Permissions

Voice to Mail may request microphone access in order to record voice input for transcription. This permission is necessary for the main function of the app.

Children

Voice to Mail is not specifically directed at children. The app is intended as a general productivity tool.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
